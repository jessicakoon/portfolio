Driving digital revenue for one of America's most recognized art brands — Thomas Kinkade Studios — through performance marketing, email campaigns, and creative production.

At Art Brand Studios, I served as both a designer and digital marketing strategist supporting the Thomas Kinkade Studios brand. Working cross-functionally with the sales, licensing, and creative teams, I planned and executed multi-channel digital campaigns aimed at growing online revenue, re-engaging lapsed collectors, and acquiring new customers across paid and owned channels.

The Thomas Kinkade brand has 70+ licensees and distributes artwork through an international gallery network. My work focused on digital touchpoints — turning that legacy brand equity into measurable online performance.

What I Built

Campaign work across paid, owned, and creative channels.

Campaign 01

Paid Social — Meta & Instagram

Developed and tested creative assets (static, carousel, video) for Meta and Instagram advertising, targeting collector segments and lookalike audiences. Managed A/B testing across ad copy, creative formats, and landing pages to optimize for ROAS and conversion rate.

Meta Ads Instagram A/B Testing Creative Production
Campaign 02

Email Marketing — Klaviyo

Designed and executed email campaigns in Klaviyo for seasonal promotions, new collection launches, and collector re-engagement flows. Built automated sequences including welcome series, browse abandon, and post-purchase follow-ups. Optimized subject lines and send timing to improve open and click rates.

Klaviyo Automation Segmentation Email Design
Campaign 03

Google Search — SEM

Set up and managed Google Ads campaigns targeting brand and category keywords for Thomas Kinkade artwork, limited editions, and collectibles. Wrote ad copy, managed bidding strategy, and tracked conversion through Google Analytics to inform ongoing optimization.

Google Ads SEM Keyword Strategy Analytics
Campaign 04

Seasonal Campaigns — Holiday & Limited Editions

Led creative production for holiday and limited-edition launch campaigns across email, paid social, and landing pages. Coordinated timelines with the sales and licensing teams to align creative delivery with product availability and gallery launch events.

Seasonal Limited Edition Multi-Channel Production
Campaign 05

Organic Social Content

Created branded content for Thomas Kinkade Studios social channels — designing posts, stories, and reels that balanced the brand's heritage aesthetic with formats that perform on Instagram and Facebook. Managed content calendar alongside broader campaign activations.

Instagram Facebook Content Strategy Adobe Creative Suite
Campaign 06

Analytics & Performance Reporting

Maintained campaign dashboards in Salesforce and Google Analytics, delivering weekly and monthly performance reports to stakeholders. Tracked metrics including CTR, CPM, ROAS, email open rate, and revenue attribution — using insights to guide creative and budget decisions.

Salesforce Google Analytics Reporting ROI Optimization

Process

How campaigns went from brief to live.

Step 01

Strategy & Audience Research

Defined campaign goals, target audience segments (existing collectors, new acquirers, gift buyers), and channel mix based on historical performance data and seasonal calendar. Used Salesforce CRM data and Meta Audience Insights to shape targeting parameters.

Step 02

Creative Concepting & Production

Designed all campaign creative in Adobe Creative Suite — adapting Thomas Kinkade's luminous, painterly aesthetic into digital-native formats (stories, carousels, animated ads, email headers). Maintained strict brand consistency across all touchpoints while optimizing for platform-specific dimensions and best practices.

Step 03

Campaign Launch & A/B Testing

Launched campaigns across Meta, Google, and Klaviyo with structured A/B tests — varying creative formats, copy angles, subject lines, and CTAs. Monitored daily performance metrics and made real-time budget adjustments to favor top-performing variants.

Step 04

Reporting & Optimization

Compiled weekly performance summaries and monthly deep-dive reports for the marketing and leadership teams. Flagged underperforming placements, surfaced creative insights, and fed learnings back into the next campaign cycle — creating a continuous improvement loop.
