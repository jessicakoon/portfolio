UX / UI Case Study

Twitch × Adobe Creative Jams

Role
UI / UX Designer
Platform
Desktop Website
Tools
Adobe XD, Illustrator
Launch
March 2021

Overview

Twitch communities are made up of diehard fans who learn about their passions in many different forms. Live streaming is one way to tap into knowledge — but how can that sharing become truly omnidirectional? How can a baking method from a French cookbook reach someone searching specifically for how to bake macarons?

The brief: design a third-party desktop website that serves as a single-stop resource for any media related to a topic, encouraging users to find new interests, events and communities.

82% of Twitch's audience is male, and 55% of viewers fall between 18–34 — the platform needed to feel built for every livestreamer, not just the loudest one.

Process

  • 01Brainstorm and sketch out wireframes
  • 02Iterate on visual and interaction design
  • 03Conduct user testing to stress-test the new component
  • 04Upload assets and finalize the layout

Main challenges

A 24-hour build window, a 3-person team, and the need to encourage fans to create, share and discover clips, notes and chapters of content — while uplifting streamers from underrepresented communities.

About

A multidisciplinary designer and marketing strategist.

I'm Jessica Koon, based in the Bay Area. My work sits at the intersection of design, storytelling and performance marketing — where thoughtful visuals meet measurable results.

With a background in design studies and several years across creative production, digital marketing and licensing, I've developed a strong understanding of how design supports business goals. At Art Brand Studios, I helped manage creative production for globally recognized brands including Disney and Warner Bros., while leading marketing initiatives that increased campaign performance and drove measurable revenue growth.

Growing up in a multicultural environment shaped how I see design: as a universal language that connects people and ideas across cultures.

I'm passionate about building design systems and visual experiences that are both aesthetically refined and strategically effective — blending minimalism, typography and user-centered thinking with data, from optimizing landing pages to preparing production-ready artwork for large-scale distribution.

Outside of work, I enjoy travel writing, photography, and creating product content as a UGC creator — always exploring new ways brands can connect with their audiences through authentic visual narratives.
